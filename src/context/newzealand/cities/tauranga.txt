Tauranga is a coastal city in the Bay of Plenty region of the North Island, known for its stunning beaches and outdoor activities.
Relax on the beautiful beaches of Mount Maunganui and enjoy swimming or sunbathing.
Take a hike to the top of Mount Maunganui for panoramic views of the bay.
Visit McLaren Falls Park for picnicking, walking trails, and waterfalls.
Explore the charming Mount Maunganui town center with its boutique shops and cafes.
Take a boat trip or go diving to explore the marine life around Mayor Island or White Island.
