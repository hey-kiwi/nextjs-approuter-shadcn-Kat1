Napier is a city in the Hawke's Bay region of the North Island, famous for its Art Deco architecture, vineyards, and cultural events.
Discover the Art Deco architecture during a self-guided walking tour or guided tour.
Visit the National Aquarium of New Zealand and observe marine life up close.
Explore the wineries and vineyards of the Hawke's Bay region and enjoy wine tasting.
Attend the annual Art Deco Festival held in February, featuring vintage cars, fashion shows, and jazz music.
Relax on the beaches of Marine Parade or take a bike ride along the scenic Hawke's Bay Trails.
