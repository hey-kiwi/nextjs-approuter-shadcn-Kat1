Wellington, the capital city, is located at the southern tip of the North Island. It offers a vibrant arts scene, picturesque harbor, and a thriving café culture.
Explore the eclectic Cuba Street, known for its shops, cafes, and live music scene.
Visit Te Papa Museum, New Zealand's national museum, showcasing art, history, and natural treasures.
Take a ride on the Wellington Cable Car for stunning views and visit the Wellington Botanic Garden.
Enjoy the café culture and try Wellington's renowned coffee.
Visit the Zealandia sanctuary, a haven for native birds and wildlife.
