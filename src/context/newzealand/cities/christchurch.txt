Christchurch is the largest city on the South Island and known for its English heritage, beautiful gardens, and post-earthquake rebuild.
Explore the picturesque Botanic Gardens and the tranquil Hagley Park.
Visit the International Antarctic Centre and experience Antarctic simulations and penguin encounters.
Discover the history and exhibits at the Canterbury Museum.
Take a punting tour on the Avon River or enjoy a leisurely walk along its banks.
Explore the street art and creative spaces in the city's rejuvenated central district.
