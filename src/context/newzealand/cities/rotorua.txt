Rotorua is a city in the North Island known for its geothermal wonders, Māori culture, and outdoor adventures.
Explore the geothermal parks like Wai-O-Tapu and Te Puia to witness geysers, hot springs, and mud pools.
Experience a traditional Māori cultural performance and enjoy a Hangi feast.
Visit the Redwoods Forest for hiking, mountain biking, and canopy walks.
Relax in natural hot pools and indulge in wellness treatments at one of the spas.
Take a scenic helicopter ride or enjoy a thrilling zipline experience.
