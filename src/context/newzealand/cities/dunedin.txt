Dunedin is a city known for its Scottish heritage, historic architecture, and vibrant student culture due to the University of Otago.
Explore the beautiful Baldwin Street, the steepest residential street in the world.
Visit the Olveston Historic Home and gain insights into the city's rich history.
Take a wildlife cruise to spot seals, penguins, and albatrosses along the Otago Peninsula.
Discover the artistic side of Dunedin at the Dunedin Public Art Gallery.
Enjoy a scenic drive along the stunning Otago coastline to visit the Moeraki Boulders.
