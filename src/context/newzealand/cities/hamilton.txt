Hamilton is a city located in the Waikato region of the North Island, known for its beautiful gardens and vibrant arts scene.
Visit the Hamilton Gardens, featuring themed gardens from different cultures.
Explore the Waikato Museum, showcasing art, history, and interactive exhibits.
Take a scenic boat ride on the Waikato River or try kayaking or paddleboarding.
Enjoy a walk along the picturesque Hamilton Lake or indulge in bird watching.
Attend a performance at the Hamilton Performing Arts Centre or visit local art galleries.
